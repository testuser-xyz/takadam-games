# Takadam Learning Games Hub

## Platform Overview
The **Takadam Learning Games Hub** is a child-friendly, interactive educational platform designed to make learning foundational skills fun and engaging. Built with vibrant colors, accessible UI, and smooth animations, the hub serves as a central menu for various educational minigames. 

To ensure a structured and supervised learning environment, all games on the platform are secured via a simple access code (default: `1234`). 

---

## Games & Functionality

### 1. Coin Counting Adventure
**Educational Focus:** Mathematics (Money Recognition, Counting, and Sorting)

**How it Works:**
- **Objective:** Teach children how to recognize different coins and understand their values.
- **Gameplay:** Players are presented with sparkling coins of different denominations. They must evaluate the coins and drag/sort them into the correct corresponding jars or target areas based on their value.
- **Outcomes:** Enhances early addition skills and real-world currency recognition.

### 2. Cookie Sharing Fun
**Educational Focus:** Mathematics (Basic Division and Fair Sharing)

**How it Works:**
- **Objective:** Introduce the concept of division in a relatable, visual way.
- **Gameplay:** Players are given a specific number of delicious cookies and an even number of friends. They need to distribute the cookies evenly among the friends so that everyone gets the exact same amount.
- **Outcomes:** Builds foundational arithmetic logic regarding splitting and fractions before formal math is introduced.

### 3. My Pride Drawing
**Educational Focus:** Creativity, Expression, and Motor Skills

**How it Works:**
- **Objective:** Give children a blank canvas to freely express themselves, driven by the prompt: *"What toy makes you feel super happy? Draw it below, then write your name!"*
- **Gameplay:** 
  - Players are provided with an interactive 400x400 drawing canvas that supports both mouse (desktop) and touch (tablet/mobile) inputs.
  - Features a custom palette with 6 bright colors (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple) and an eraser tool to make corrections.
  - A "Clear Canvas" button prompts a confirmation to wipe the board clean so they can start over.
- **Outcomes:** Encourages emotional expression and helps develop fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination.
